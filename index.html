Daily Tips
🍬
Beat Sugar Cravings
Cravings peak at 20 minutes then fade. Drink water, take a walk, or chew gum — the urge usually passes on its own.
🔥
The 500 Calorie Rule
A daily 500 cal deficit creates ~0.5 kg (1 lb) of fat loss per week. Small, consistent deficits beat crash diets every time.
💧
Drink Before Eating
500 ml of water 30 minutes before a meal naturally reduces hunger and can cut calorie intake by up to 13%.
🥗
Eat Protein First
Starting meals with protein keeps you full longer and reduces the total calories you eat — without counting every bite.
